About HardFort

Forging the future of physical building security.

Our History & Mission

Established in 2023, HardFort Security was founded on a bedrock of expertise. While the company is young, it is built upon over 40 years of experience in upgrading physical security for Critical National Infrastructure (CNI) sites.

We specialize in the installation and maintenance of high-security physical barriers. From standard steel doors to high-security rated localized hardening, we bring a level of precision and reliability usually reserved for government and critical utility sectors.

Our mission is simple: to bring building-grade hardening to commercial and high-risk client sites nationwide, ensuring that your physical perimeter is as secure as the assets it protects.
